Animation Project

I created an animation of a beautiful scenary which contains a boat moving along the waves of the ocean, using Adobe Animate

Animation Project

Embarking on the creation of a boat animation was as much an odyssey as the vessel's own journey across the waves. It was a venture filled with learning and discovery, guided by the beacon of a helpful tutorial when Adobe Animate's complexities seemed like uncharted waters. The result is a tranquil scene of a boat gliding across the sea, its path charted by my newfound understanding of motion tweening and guided by a crafted motion path that mimics the ocean's rhythm. The visual depth achieved with layered vector images, and the hand-drawn sea painted in gradients of blue, brought the seascape to life. The boat, set to navigate behind rolling waves, adds a touch of realism to the animation, creating an immersive experience. Complementing the visuals is a soundscape where a voiceover and the subtle background of ocean waves converge, ensuring the narration stands clear, like a captain's command over the gentle sea. This animation not only showcases the skills I've developed but also the storytelling power of digital artistry—a captivating addition to my portfolio.
